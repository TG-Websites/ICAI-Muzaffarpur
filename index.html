About Muzaffarpur Branch

Learn more about the values, mission, and services of the Muzaffarpur Branch of CIRC of ICAI.

The Muzaffarpur Branch of CIRC of ICAI plays a vital role in shaping the future of the accounting profession in the region. We are committed to providing quality professional education, guidance, and support to our members and students. We aim to foster an environment of learning, ethical practice, and professional development. Our branch also strives to enhance the knowledge base of the accounting community through various initiatives, events, and programs.

Mission & Values

  • Provide exceptional education and training for members and students.
  • Promote ethical practices and professional excellence in all fields of accounting.
  • Encourage networking, collaboration, and skill development among professionals.
  • Support the growth of the accounting profession in Muzaffarpur and beyond.

A Message From Chairperson

It is indeed a great honour and responsibility to chair the Darbhanga Branch of CIRC of ICAI. I am grateful to Darbhanga Branch Team for showing their trust and faith by electing me as the Chairman of Darbhanga Branch of CIRC of ICAI. I, along with my team, am committed to putting the best efforts for the benefit of our members & students of our branch in turn to elevate this Branch to greater heights.

Darbhanga Branch is committed to the acceleration of various programmes for both members, students, and the public at large, thereby giving our nation the best and skilled professionals. Professional education opens up an altogether different world, and new innovative ideas bring enormous transformative power for any given crisis.

Everyone deserves the best start in life, and that won’t be possible without quality, values, and innovative thinking. If we need our future to be just and sustainable, we have to think collectively.

Beginning my term on the strong pillars of ‘Professionalism, Excellence, and Innovation’, with ethics and values being an integral part of the system. We believe that these values – ethics & integrity are the foundations of our profession and for members to follow in all walks of life.

I am expecting many milestones to be achieved, and there is a desire to serve our noble profession with even more positivity and enthusiasm.

With Regards,
Naval Kumar Mishra
Chairman
