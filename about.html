About Us

Muzaffarpur Branch of CIRC of ICAI

The Muzaffarpur Branch Of CIRC of ICAI is a statutory body established under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1984 for the regulation of the profession of Chartered Accountants in India.

Muzaffarpur Branch of CIRC of ICAI has been empowering accountants since 2024 with a dynamic range of academic programs, workshops, and collaborative engagements with local bodies and associations.

The branch was inaugurated by then Hon'ble President Shri CA Ranjeet Kumar Agarwal on 16th August 2024. It is situated in Muzaffarpur City, Bihar, covering Muzaffarpur, Madhubani, and Samastipur, with 200+ members and 2400+ students associated.

The branch regularly hosts academic and other programs, including seminars, workshops, and meetings.
